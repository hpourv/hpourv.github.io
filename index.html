About Me

I am currently a M.Sc. student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Babol Noshirvani University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Ali Aghagolzadeh.

I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from Babol Noshirvani University of Technology in September 2022. During my undergraduate, I worked with Prof. Ali Aghagolzadeh on Differentially Private Logistic Regression.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the areas of Learning Theory and Information Theory. Specifically, my research is currently centered on Probabilistic Modelling, Diffusion Models, Differential Privacy, Algorithmic Fairness, and Machine Unlearning.

Feel free to email me if you come across any research interests we may have in common. I am always open to potential collaborations.
