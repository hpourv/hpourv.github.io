About Me

I am currently an M.Sc. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Babol Noshirvani University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Ali Aghagolzadeh. I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering from Babol Noshirvani University of Technology in September 2022. During my undergraduate final project, I worked with Prof. Ali Aghagolzadeh on Differentially Private ML models.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the areas of Learning Theory, Generative Models, Trustworthy ML, and Information Theory. Specifically, my research is currently centered on Diffusion Models.
